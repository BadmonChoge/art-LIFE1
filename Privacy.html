Privacy Policy

Art-Life respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you engage with our website or participate in any of our programs and services.

Quick Navigation
Information Collection Use of Information Data Protection Your Rights Contact Us

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you engage with Art-Life.

We collect information through various interactions:

  • Direct Interactions: When you sign up for newsletters, contact us through forms, or register for events
  • Automated Technologies: Technical data like IP addresses, browser type, and device information through cookies
  • Third Parties: Information from social media platforms when you interact with our content
  • Public Sources: Information available publicly about your artistic work or community involvement
Personal Data
Name, email address, phone number, and contact preferences
Professional Data
Artistic background, portfolio information, and career interests
Technical Data
IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns
Engagement Data
Event participation, program involvement, and feedback responses

2. Use of Information

How we use your information to serve our community and advance our mission.

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Program Delivery: To provide access to our events, workshops, and community programs
  • Communication: To send updates, newsletters, and important announcements about opportunities
  • Community Building: To connect artists, foster collaborations, and build networks
  • Improvement: To enhance our website, programs, and services based on user feedback
  • Compliance: To meet legal obligations and protect the rights of our community

Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, or legal obligations. We never use your information for purposes unrelated to Art-Life's mission without your explicit consent.

3. Data Protection

Our commitment to keeping your information safe and secure.

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Technical Safeguards: Encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits
  • Organizational Measures: Limited access to authorized personnel only and regular staff training
  • Physical Security: Secure storage facilities for any physical records
  • Third-Party Oversight: Careful vetting of service providers who handle data on our behalf

Data Retention: We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes collected, in accordance with our data retention policy and legal requirements.

Data Sharing: We never sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted partners only when necessary for program delivery or with your explicit consent.

4. Your Rights

You have control over your personal data. Here are your rights.

Under data protection laws, you have the following rights:

Right to Access
Request copies of your personal data that we hold
Right to Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances
Right to Restrict
Request restriction of processing your data
Right to Portability
Request transfer of your data to another organization
Right to Object
Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond to your request within 30 days and handle it in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
