Urban Expressions Exhibition

Exhibition Monthly Series
Dates: October 15-30, 2023
Hours: 10AM - 6PM Daily
Capacity: 45/100 spots filled
Cost: Free admission

Overview

Explore the dynamic relationship between art and urban environments through this immersive exhibition featuring street art, photography, and mixed media installations from Nairobi's most creative neighborhoods.

Featured Artists

  • Jamal Mwangi - Street photography documenting Nairobi's evolving cityscape
  • Sarah Omondi - Large-scale graffiti art exploring social commentary
  • David Kimani - Mixed media installations using found urban materials
  • Fatma Hassan - Urban landscape painting series "City in Transition"

Special Events During Exhibition

  • Opening Reception: October 15, 6PM-9PM (RSVP required)
  • Artist Talk: October 22, 3PM - Meet the creators
  • Guided Tour: October 28, 11AM - Curator-led walkthrough
  • Community Dialogue: October 29, 2PM - "Art in Public Spaces"

Location

Art-Life Gallery, 123 Creative Street, Nairobi

Accessible venue with wheelchair ramps and accessible facilities

Who Should Attend

Art enthusiasts, urban planners, photographers, students, and anyone interested in contemporary African art and urban culture. Family-friendly.

Registration

Free admission. Registration recommended for opening night and special events to ensure seating.
