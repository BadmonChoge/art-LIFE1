Mission

To identify needs, develop talents, create opportunities, promote high impact art-focused programs and festival events for Kenyans and the visiting global audience.

Identify Needs

We actively research and understand the unique challenges faced by artists and creative communities.

Develop Talents

Providing training, mentorship, and resources to nurture the next generation of creative leaders.

Create Opportunities

Building pathways for artists to showcase their work, connect with audiences, and sustain their practice.

Vision

To be the premier and lead promoter of art-cultural programs and events in Kenya and beyond.

Art-Life is committed to accessibility, equity & accountability. Through our innovative arts programs, inclusive spaces & dedicated staff, we are working to create an environment where all of our community feels inspired, celebrated & value driven.

Accessibility

Ensuring arts and culture are accessible to everyone, regardless of background, ability, or circumstance.

Equity

Creating fair opportunities and removing barriers for underrepresented groups in the arts.

Accountability

Maintaining transparency in our work and being accountable to our community and stakeholders.
