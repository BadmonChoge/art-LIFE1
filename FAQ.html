General Information

Art-Life is dedicated to fostering creativity, supporting emerging artists, and making art accessible to all. We bridge the gap between artists and communities through exhibitions, workshops, and public programs that transform lives through creative expression.

Art-Life was established in 2015 by a collective of Nairobi-based artists and has since grown into one of Kenya's most vibrant arts organizations, reaching over 5,000 artists and 15+ communities across the country.

Participation

There are several ways to get involved:

  • Attend our open calls for artists and exhibitions
  • Join our volunteer program for community projects
  • Participate in community workshops and skill-building sessions
  • Subscribe to our newsletter for updates on opportunities
  • Become a member for exclusive access and benefits

Events & Exhibitions

Approximately 80% of our events are completely free to ensure accessibility for all. For special ticketed exhibitions or workshops, we offer:

  • Discounted rates for students, seniors, and artists
  • Free community tickets (available through our outreach program)
  • Early-bird pricing for members
  • Group discounts for schools and community organizations
  • Pay-what-you-can options for financial accessibility

We maintain a vibrant exhibition calendar throughout the year:

  • Major thematic exhibitions quarterly (approx. 3 months each)
  • Pop-up shows and community exhibitions monthly
  • Ongoing digital exhibitions on our website and social media
  • Special annual events like our Art-Life Festival in October
  • Traveling exhibitions in partnership with community centers
  • Emerging artist showcases every 6 months

Support & Funding

We operate through a diversified funding model to ensure sustainability and independence:

  • Corporate sponsorships and partnerships (40%)
  • Government arts grants and cultural funding (25%)
  • Individual donations and membership contributions (20%)
  • Revenue from select ticketed events and workshops (10%)
  • Foundation grants and philanthropic support (5%)

All funds are transparently allocated to programs, artist support, and community initiatives.
