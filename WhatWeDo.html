Defining Us

Art-Life transforms communities through creative expression. Our comprehensive programs empower artists, foster innovation, and build sustainable creative economies that uplift Nairobi's neighborhoods and beyond.

Our Driving Vision

Our strong drive for the industry is to spearhead research, organize incubation programs through training and mentorship, curate and promote legacy art-cultural festivals and events that support community-engaged artists to break new ground for Nairobi's creative economy; and transform the city's poor suburbs into vibrant, friendly and dynamic neighborhoods; that widen opportunities for wealth and job creation.

Training Programs

Offers short- and long-term programs tailored to diverse artistic skill levels. In partnership with the University of Nairobi, Art-Life hosts an annual Symposium and monthly "Let's Talk Art" workshops.

Key Features:

  • Annual Symposium with University of Nairobi
  • Monthly "Let's Talk Art" workshops
  • Specialized programs for women and PWDs
  • Business skills for creative entrepreneurs
  • Community artivist development
Youth Focus Women Empowerment Community Building

Arts Innovation Hub

Supports emerging artists through mentorship, market access, and entrepreneurial training. Focused on women, disabled, and marginalized youth, it helps artists turn creative ideas into viable ventures.

Incubation Support:

  • One-on-one mentorship programs
  • Market access and networking
  • Entrepreneurial skills training
  • University expert guidance
  • Business development support
Innovation Women Focus Marginalized Groups

Art-Life Wellness Center

In collaboration with the University of Nairobi, provides affordable mental health support to artists. Promotes art as therapy and includes clinical music programs for non-pharmacological mental health rehabilitation.

Wellness Services:

  • Affordable mental health support
  • Art therapy programs
  • Clinical music therapy
  • Non-pharmacological rehabilitation
  • Artist wellness workshops
Mental Health Community Support Innovative Therapy

Art-Life Musical Academy

A one-year program offering boot camps in songwriting, vocals, and instrumentation. It fuses Kenyan traditional and modern music, encouraging storytelling and artistic collaboration for local and global impact.

Program Features:

  • One-year comprehensive program
  • Songwriting and vocal training
  • Instrumentation workshops
  • Traditional-modern music fusion
  • Global collaboration opportunities
Cultural Fusion Youth Development Cultural Preservation

Digital Arts Program

Equips artists with digital tools and AI techniques, enhancing competitiveness in animation, gaming, and crypto art. It fosters innovation and unlocks global creative economies through co-creation and tech-driven expression.

Digital Skills:

  • AI-assisted creative tools
  • Digital collaboration platforms

Festival Programs

Art-Life hosts festivals to spotlight emerging artists through live events, workshops, and cultural collaborations. Flagship events include the Nairobi Art-Life Festival, Nature-Fest (climate-focused), and West'A Fest (cycling and arts).

Flagship Events:

  • Nairobi Art-Life Festival
  • Nature-Fest (climate focus)
  • West'A Fest (cycling & arts)
  • Live workshops and exhibitions
  • Cultural exchange programs
Community Events Cultural Innovation Youth Engagement

Fashion Innovation

Merging Kenya's traditional fashion with modern design and AI, this program revives cultural aesthetics using local materials. Young designers explore the country's 45 ethnic groups to inspire innovative fashion rooted in heritage.

Design Focus:

  • Traditional-modern fusion
  • AI-assisted design tools
  • Local material innovation
  • Cultural heritage exploration
  • Sustainable fashion practices
Cultural Innovation Tech Integration Designer Development

Hand-craft Programs

Revitalizes traditional crafts like pottery, beadwork, and textiles into scalable cottage industries. The program promotes artisanal innovation, tech integration, and connects crafts to wider industries like decor and events.

Craft Focus:

  • Traditional craft revitalization
  • Cottage industry development
  • Tech integration in crafts
  • Market expansion strategies
  • Inter-industry collaboration
Artisan Support Traditional Innovation Women Artisans

Arts Residency

A creative space offering mentorship, co-creation, and cultural exchange for local and international artists. It nurtures at-risk creators while building bridges across communities and global arts scenes.

Residency Features:

  • Mentorship from established artists
  • Co-creation spaces and studios
  • International cultural exchange
  • At-risk creator support
  • Community integration programs
